AKAIO 1.9.0

The carts which support AKAIO 1.9.0 can be found here:

  • Acekard 2i
  • Acekard 2/2.1
  • Acekard RPG - WoodRPG 1.62 may be a better choice for this cart.

Installation is simple:

  • Download AKAIO 1.9.0 from here: https://flashcard-archive.ds-homebrew.com/acekard.com/AceKard_2i/AKAIO_1.9.0.zip
  • Extract it with your preferred file extractor
  • Copy the extracted files to the root of your micro SD card

If your cart is not working as expected, see the troubleshooting page here: Troubleshooting
