R4 / M3 Simply (Wood 1.62)

These carts only supports micro SD cards up to 2 GB in capacity

The original R4 and M3 Simply will use Wood 1.62, this kernel has great game compatibility! The major downside to these carts is that they are limited up to 2GB microSD cards as they do not support SDHC cards.

Installation is simple:

  • Download the kernel from here: flashcard-archive link
  • Extract it using your preferred file extractor
  • Copy the kernel files to your micro SD card

If your cart is not working as expected, see the troubleshooting page here: Troubleshooting
