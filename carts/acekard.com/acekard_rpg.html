Acekard RPG

The Acekard RPG was one of the few carts which was supported by the team behind the Wood kernel!

Installation is simple:

  • Download WoodRPG 1.62 from here: flashcard-archive link
  • Extract it with your preferred file extractor
  • Copy the extracted files to the root of your micro SD card

If your cart is not working as expected, see the troubleshooting page here: Troubleshooting
