7-Zip

Installation is very simple:

  • Go to https://7-zip.org
  • Download the version for your system, for most people, this will be the 64-bit version. Double check this is the case for you, especially if you have an older system!


  • Open the installer and click yes when the UAC prompt appears. It will say that the publisher is unknown, though 7-zip is safe to install onto your computer!
  • Click Install, then click close when the button changes!

Just like that, you have 7-zip installed! Now lets move onto extracting kernel files. There are a few ways to do this:
