List of carts that support BL2CK

  • r4i3d.com NEW, 2012+
  • r4igold.cc 3DS
  • r4i-gold.me 2014-2015
  • r4isdhc.hk carts with no year number, or before 2020
  • r4isdhc.com NEW/2013 carts
  • r4isdhc.com Dual-Core (no year number)
  • r4i-drive.com carts
  • R4 Ultra and Acekard 2/2.1/2i carts that fail AKAIO's clone check
  • R4iTT 3DS NEW, RTS
  • r4infinity.com Dual-Core
  • r4isdhc.in 2014
  • R4i Gold 3DS Plus (from r4ids.cn) with the faulty DS mode
  • r4-pro.com carts
  • r4dspro.com carts and the newer unlabelled clones

Installation

Installation is simple:

  • Download BL2CK from here: BL2CK_1.30.4.zip
  • Extract it
  • Copy the extracted files to your micro SD card!

If your cart is not working as expected, see the troubleshooting page here: Troubleshooting
