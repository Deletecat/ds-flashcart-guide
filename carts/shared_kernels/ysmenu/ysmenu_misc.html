Misc. YSMenu

This page is for carts which did not necessarily fit in with the other YSMenu pages. If you are on this page, it's likely because of two reasons:
a. Your cart has multiple revisions with different boot files
b. Your cart has a specific boot file for whatever reason

Installation is simple

  • Download RetroGameFan's multicart update from here: GBATemp Link
  • Extract it using your preferred file extractor
  • Go into the DSTTi Clone YSMenu folder
  • Copy all of the files in this folder to the root of your micro SD card

Now comes the fun part, finding your cart - and it's boot file - on this list!

  • Original Ace3ds from ace3ds.com - Not the Plus or the X - Make a copy of TTMenu.dat and rename it to 3DSCARD.DAT
  • R4i Gold Upgrade 1.14b from r4igold.cn - Make a copy of TTMenu.dat and rename it to R4i.dat
  • r4-king.com carts - Make a copy of TTMenu.dat and rename it to R4KING
  • R4i <New> 2011.01 from r4i-sdhc.hk - Make a copy of TTMenu.dat and rename it to config
  • r4i-rendant.com carts - Make a copy of TTMenu.dat and rename it to Rendant.dat
  • r4i-sdhc.com.au carts - Make a copy of TTMenu.dat and rename it to R4i.au
  • r4issdhc.com carts - Make two copies of TTMenu.dat and rename them to iLL.iL and R4i.PK
  • r4-v.com v1.4.x carts - Make two copies of TTMenu.dat and rename them to iLL.iL and R4i.TP

If your cart is not working as expected, see the troubleshooting page here: Troubleshooting
