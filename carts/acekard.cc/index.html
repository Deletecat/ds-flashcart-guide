Acekard.cc - Acekard 3

This is a weird cart, it's the Acekard 3! Is it affiliated with the original acekard team?

Nope.
I can't find much about the cart other than listings from a bunch of polish websites that used to sell them. Were these fakes made in Poland?
Most information I can find is on GBATemp where most of the posts are pointing out that it's a fake. Either way, it will use AKAIO 1.8.7 as it's kernel!

Installation is simple

  • Download AKAIO 1.8.7 from here: flashcard-archive link
  • Extract it using your preferred file extractor
  • Copy the extracted kernel files to the root of your micro SD card

If your cart is not working as expected, see the troubleshooting page here: Troubleshooting
