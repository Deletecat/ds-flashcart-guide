R4i-SDHC YSMenu

Here are the list of carts that are supported:

  • r4i-sdhc.com carts
  • 2014+ r4isdhc.com carts (using on other carts may brick them)
  • Any r4i-sdhc.com.tw carts that didn’t work with DSTTi-Clone YSMenu
  • Any r4i-dshc.com carts that didn’t work with DSTTi-Clone YSMenu
  • R4DSiXL3D (r4dsixl3d.com)
  • R4i Advance (r4i-advance.com)
  • R4-III(i) (r4dsl.net)
  • R4i SDHC v2.07 (r4sdhc.com)
  • r4i.cn carts
  • R4i Pocket (r4ipocket.com)
  • R4i Gold 3DS (r4i-gold.cc)
  • r4i-gold.com carts
  • r4i-gold.eu carts
  • r4i-gold.com carts without a year number
  • r4ita.com carts
  • r4i-gold.eu carts
  • r4imax.com carts
  • r4r4.be carts (r4i-sdhc variant)

Installation is simple

  1. Download Retrogamefan’s multicart update from gbatemp: https://gbatemp.net/download/retrogamefan-multi-cart-update.35737/
  2. Extract it using your preferred file extractor
  3. Go into the R4i-SDHC YSMenu folder
  4. Copy every file in this folder to the root of your micro SD card

Special Cases

In some cases, your cart may not boot after correctly installing YSMenu. To force it to boot, you will have to use a different bootloader!

The carts which may require this are:

  • r4i-sdhc.com carts
  • r4i-gold.eu carts
  • r4i-gold.com carts
  • r4imax.com carts
  • r4isdhc.com carts
  • r4r4.be carts (r4i-sdhc variant)

Only install the bootloader if your cart does not boot into YSMenu (and is still showing the ?menu message)

Installing the bootloader

To install the bootloader:

  • Download the bootloader from here: Flashcard-archive/YSMenu/DEMON_common/R4.dat
  • Put it onto your micro SD card, overwrite/replace when asked
