YSMenu - Original R4 and it's clones

This version of YSMenu is intended to be used as a 'dual boot' setup. This means that these carts boot into their own kernel first, before loading into YSMenu.

Some of these carts only support microSD cards up to 2 GB in capacity. I would suggest sticking with the stock kernel if this is the case for your cart.

Here is a list of supported carts:

  • Original R4 - Only supports up to 2GB microSD cards
  • R4-III (r4iiinew.com)
  • All carts from r4-pro.com
  • R4-III (r4dsl.net)
  • N5 (dsn5.com)
  • R4iNDSiXL (r4indsill.com)
  • R4 SDHC Christmas Edition (only this cart, other r4isdhc.com carts may brick) (r4isdhc.com)

Installation is simple

  • Follow the instructions on the previous page to get your cart's stock kernel working.
  • Download RetroGameFan's multicart update from here: GBATemp Link
  • Extract it using your preferred file extractor
  • Go into the R4 Clone YSMenu folder
  • Copy every file from this folder to the root of your micro SD card!

Booting into YSMenu

You will notice that upon booting your cart, you will be in your carts stock kernel rather than YSMenu.
Not to fear though, all you have to do once you've booted your cart is to launch YSMenu.nds!
You have to do this every time you boot your cart though so keep that in mind.

If your cart is not working as expected, see the troubleshooting page here: Troubleshooting
